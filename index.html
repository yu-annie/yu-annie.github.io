From an early age, I knew I wanted a job where I could help people when I grew up. That goal made me gravitate towards the healthcare field, and I considered becoming a surgeon, pharmacist, and everything in between. I ended up choosing to become a family nurse practitioner, a job that gave me the opportunity to work in both in-patient and out-patient settings. But I also started to see how the power of technology could improve lives as well. While working at jobs at the intersection of health and technology, I realized that I could make an even larger impact if I pursued software engineering alongside healthcare. And that's how I got to where I am today!
